About

I know what you're thinking, "Who are we?"
We are 'Project: Save Valentines', a team of dedicated individuals committed to helping you make your special day unforgettable.
Our mission is to save your Date, Friendship, Talking-stage, Marriage, Relationship, Courtship or even your Situationship by providing last-minute solutions for those who may have forgotten or overlooked important occasions.
We understand that life can get busy, and sometimes things slip through the cracks. That's where we come in!
We, however, still do offer services to those who plan for their special ones in advance.
Whether it's a thoughtful gift, a romantic gesture, a birthday gift/ surprise or a memorable experience, we have a range of services designed to help you make up for lost time and show your loved ones how much you care.



Services

We have a broad category of services which are classified into two main categories:
1. Last-minute services - for those who have forgotten or overlooked important occasions.
Services under this category include:

  • Emergency gift delivery
  • Emergency Personalized messages (Written/ Typed letter with caligraphy)
  • Quick restaurant date reservations
  • Apology services
  • Same-day flower delivery
  • Same-day cake delivery
  • And many more

We normally execute this category with utmost professionalism and speed to ensure that your special one is not disappointed. We keep confidentiality hence we do not disclose that this was a last-minute arrangement. We make it look as though it was our fault for being late and that you had planned the service ages ago.


2. Advance planning services - for those who plan for their special ones in advance.
Services under this category include:

  • Birthday planning
  • Anniversary celebrations
  • Holiday surprises
  • Special event coordination
  • Scheduled gift delivery
  • Scheduled cake delivery
  • Scheduled flower delivery
  • Personalized messages(method of delivery and how the message is written/ typed is your decision)
  • And many more

In this category, we work closely with you to understand your preferences and create a memorable experience that reflects your love and appreciation because you have given us enough time to prepare. Depending on the service, we may require a minimum of 24 hours' notice to ensure availability and proper execution. furthermore, if you choose this category, you get a discount. Percentage of the Discount depends on how early (in terms of weeks) you book our services.
